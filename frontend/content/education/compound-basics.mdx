Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world. He who understands it, earns it; he who doesn't, pays it.

A <GlossaryTooltip term="Compound Interest" definition="Interest calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest of previous periods." /> is when your money earns money, and then that money earns money too!

### The Power of Time

Starting early makes a huge difference. Even small amounts can grow significantly over decades.

**Example**: $100 invested at 7% annual return:
- After 10 years: $197
- After 20 years: $387  
- After 30 years: $761

### Key Takeaways

1. **Start early** - Time is your greatest asset
2. **Be consistent** - Regular contributions compound faster
3. **Be patient** - Compound growth accelerates over time
